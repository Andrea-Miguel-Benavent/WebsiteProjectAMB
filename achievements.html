I Junior CEU Entrepreneurship

2017 | Madrid, Spain

Junior CEU Emprende is a national contest that brings together the best entrepreneurship projects of CEU schools throughout Spain. Students, between 10 and 18 years old, present the different initiatives in which they have worked throughout the course to a jury composed of prominent personalities from the business world, many of them entrepreneurs. The tournament focuses on fulfilling three objectives: that the learning is really significant; that all projects are aimed at the common good, and the most important: create meeting points for talent.

Besides, it should be noted that to enter the final of the competition, different groups must pass an elimination test in their respective region; competition in which our group came in first place too.
